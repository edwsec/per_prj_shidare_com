2023/07/04; Inactivity

---spanish---
Solo en la inactividad nos percatamos del suelo sobre el que pisamos y 
del espacio en la que nos hallamos.

Solo la inactividad nos inicia en el misterio de la vida.

Byung-Chul Han, (2023). Vida contemplativa. Taurus.

---english---
Only in inactivity do we become aware of the ground on which we step and
of the space in which we find ourselves.

Only inactivity initiates us into the mystery of life.

Byung Chul Han, (2023). Contemplative life. Taurus.
